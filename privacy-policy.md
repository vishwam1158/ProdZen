## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Prod Zen ("we," "our," or "us"). Prod Zen is a digital wellness application designed to help you manage your screen time and build healthier phone usage habits.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. This policy complies with Google Play Store requirements and applicable data protection laws.

**By using Prod Zen, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.**

## 3. Permissions We Request

### 3.1 Usage Access Permission (Required)
- **What it accesses:** App usage statistics from Android UsageStatsManager
- **Why we need it:** To display your screen time data and enforce app limits
- **What we DON'T access:** Individual app content, messages, or personal data

### 3.2 Accessibility Service (Required)
- **What it accesses:** Foreground app package names only
- **Why we need it:** To detect when you open monitored apps and show mindful intervention screens
- **What we DON'T access:** 
  - Screen content or text
  - Keyboard inputs or passwords
  - Messages, emails, or communications
  - Browser URLs or web content
  - Files or documents

**Important:** Our accessibility service is configured with `canRetrieveWindowContent="false"`, meaning we CANNOT read any text or content on your screen.

### 3.3 Notification Permission (Optional)
- **What it does:** Allows us to send local notifications
- **Why we need it:** Focus session reminders, achievement alerts, and daily goal notifications
- **What we DON'T do:** We never send marketing or promotional notifications

### 3.4 Overlay Permission (Required for interventions)
- **What it does:** Allows displaying screens over other apps
- **Why we need it:** To show mindful pause screens when you open tracked apps
- **What we DON'T do:** We never display ads or unwanted content

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

1. **Providing Core Functionality**
   - Displaying your screen time statistics
   - Showing mindful intervention screens
   - Tracking focus session progress
   - Managing app time limits

2. **Personalization**
   - Customizing intervention screen appearance
   - Remembering your preferences
   - Suggesting intentions based on past entries

3. **Analytics & Improvements**
   - Identifying and fixing app crashes (Firebase Crashlytics)

4. **Gamification**

   - Maintaining daily streaks

## 5. Data Storage & Security

### 5.1 Local Storage
**All your personal data is stored exclusively on your device.** We use Android's Room database for secure local storage.

### 5.2 What We Do NOT Do
- ❌ We do NOT upload your usage data to any server
- ❌ We do NOT share your data with third parties for advertising
- ❌ We do NOT sell your data
- ❌ We do NOT create user profiles on external servers
- ❌ We do NOT track your location
- ❌ We do NOT access your contacts, messages, or photos

### 5.3 Firebase Services
We use Firebase services that operate according to Google's privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Data Collected |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Firebase Crashlytics | Crash reporting | Anonymous crash logs, device info |
| Firebase Analytics | App improvement | Anonymous usage events |

Firebase data is anonymized and cannot be linked to you personally. For more information, see [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
